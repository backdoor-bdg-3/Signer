About Backdoor Web Signer

The ultimate iOS app signing solution

Our Mission

Backdoor Web Signer was created to provide a simple, efficient, and reliable way to sign iOS applications. We understand the challenges developers face when distributing their apps outside the App Store, and we're here to make that process as smooth as possible.

What We Offer

  • Fast and reliable iOS app signing
  • Support for apps of any size (1MB to 10GB+)
  • Secure processing with immediate file cleanup
  • User-friendly interface with step-by-step guidance
  • No registration required

Technology

Backdoor Web Signer uses zsign, a powerful iOS app re-signing utility that works directly with your certificates and provisioning profiles. Our web interface makes this technology accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Security & Privacy

We take your security seriously. All uploaded files are processed securely and deleted immediately after signing. We never store your certificates, provisioning profiles, or passwords. Your data remains yours alone.

Key Features

iOS App Signing

Sign iOS apps of any size with your own certificates and provisioning profiles.

OTA Installation

Install signed apps directly on your iOS device using QR codes or direct links.

App Icon Customization

Replace the app icon with your own custom image during the signing process.

Entitlements Management

Extract, view, and select entitlements from your IPA and provisioning profile for precise control.

Dylib Injection

Inject dynamic libraries into your apps to extend functionality or modify behavior.

Bundle Customization

Modify bundle ID, name, and version to customize the app identity during signing.
