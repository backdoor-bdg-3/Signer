Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, Backdoor Web Signer is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs.

Do you store my certificates or provisioning profiles?

No, we do not store any of your files. All uploaded files are processed immediately and then deleted from our servers.

What's the maximum file size I can upload?

Backdoor Web Signer supports files up to 10GB in size, making it suitable for even the largest iOS applications.

How long does the signing process take?

The signing process is very fast, typically taking just a few seconds for small apps and a few minutes for larger apps.

Can I sign apps for distribution on the App Store?

No, this service is intended for re-signing apps for personal use or enterprise distribution. For App Store distribution, you should use Apple's official tools.
